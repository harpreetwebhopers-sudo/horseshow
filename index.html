ABOUT THE INITIATIVE

Reviving a Lost
Pride of Punjab

The Punjab Horse Heritage Show is more than an event, it is a movement to bring back recognition, respect, and revival to the rare and historic Punjab Horse breed. Once an integral part of Punjab’s royal, cultural, and agricultural legacy, these horses deserve a platform where their strength, elegance, and lineage can be celebrated again.

This two-day showcase aims to:

verified_user
Encourage horse owners to preserve authentic Punjab horse bloodlines.
public
Create awareness among the public and younger generations
account_balance
Build a sustainable future for this endangered breed.
